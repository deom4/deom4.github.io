Hi, my name is Dayeon Eom and I am a Ph.D. student in the department of Life Sciences Communication at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

My research interest involves public health and science communication, specifically regarding how to better communicate with marginalized communities.

I completed my B.S. and M.S. in journalism at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. And since then, I have worked for various media publications including Investigate Midwest, The Nevada Indepenedent and The Indianapolis Star.
